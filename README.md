# NeeVLY

NeeVLY is a hyperlocal digital procurement and coordination platform concept focused on simplifying fragmented local sourcing and operational workflows.

## Current Areas of Exploration

* Buyer and supplier coordination
* Local discovery systems
* Product and store architecture
* Order workflows
* Role-based onboarding
* UI/UX systems
* Operational simplification through technology

## Status

Currently in early-stage design and MVP exploration phase.

## Screens Currently Being Explored

* Authentication flow
* Buyer home experience
* Store/product pages
* Product discovery
* Order tracking systems
* Role selection flow
